About Our Education

Pottersville Private School is a top-flight school with reputation for excellence in academic performance, moral standards and co-curricular achievements. You will find us amongst the top-rated school in the Nigeria.

Our multi-curriculum teaching platform, which comes with a deliberate blend of Nigerian, British and American curriculums, puts our students on a pedestal for international education opportunities. In addition to that, our dedication to quality academic standards and wholesome character build-up of our students reflects on why our students excel at various external examinations.

We have a global approach to teaching and nurturing. We connect students to opportunities. We believe in the “Total Care” approach which is why we go further during external admissions process to assist our students through our experienced placement counsellors.

We appreciate your interest in our school and trust that our website will guide you in making the best choice for your child’s educational future. We look forward to welcoming your child to our Pottersville Private School.
